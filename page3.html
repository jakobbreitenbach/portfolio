11lvl Druid(Moon)
The character I'm playing currently is a Kalashtar moon druid. The group I was joining needed a wise character as the group had a problem with failing wisdom saves so I decided to play a race with advantage on those saves and a class that focuses on wisdom. The racial ability of Kalashtar to speak telepathically has also been useful to communicate in with my pary while I'm in wildshape.
